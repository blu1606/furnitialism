# UX & Technical Strategy: Smart 3D Interior

## Section 1: Slide Deck Outline

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Headline:** Smart 3D Interior: Redefining Furniture Commerce
- **Subtitle:** Leveraging AI and Spatial Design to bridge the gap between digital browsing and physical reality.

### Slide 2: The Context: Beyond the Flat Catalog
- **Problem:** Traditional 2D e-commerce lacks scale, material depth, and contextual visualization.
- **The Gap:** 70% of furniture returns are due to "size/style mismatch" in the home.
- **The Solution:** Immersive "Digital Twin" environments powered by Smart 3D Interior.

### Slide 3: The Science of Interaction (Core UX Principles)
- **Fitts's Law:** Large 3D hit-boxes and floating interactive price tags for effortless selection.
- **Hick's Law:** AI-curated room selections to reduce decision paralysis.
- **Jakob's Law:** Familiar e-commerce patterns (Cart, Checkout) integrated into a 3D space.
- **Tesler's Law:** System-managed complex 3D navigation via intelligent Camera Sway and Auto-focus.

### Slide 4: The Immersive Journey (User Flow)
- **Discovery (3D Scene):** Interactive exploration with hover highlights and real-time lighting.
- **AI-Enhanced Detail:** Focused product data presented via minimalist Glassmorphism overlays.
- **Frictionless Conversion:** "Cart Orb" animation provides instant visual feedback of actions.

### Slide 5: The AI Edge: Scanning & Forge
- **AI Assistant:** Natural language search and automated camera orchestration.
- **Visual Scanning:** Real-time mesh analysis simulation with signature #7C3AED violet pulses.
- **The Forge:** The industry's first image-to-3D reconstruction pipeline mockup.

### Slide 6: The Future Roadmap
- **Spatial Audio:** Attention-guiding 3D audio for deeper immersion.
- **Mobile Optimization:** AR-view "portal" mode for on-site visualization.
- **Spatial Computing:** Readiness for Vision Pro and Quest 3 ecosystems.
